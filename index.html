About Us

Welcome to DogsHaven, your ultimate destination for professional dog grooming services. At DogHaven, we are passionate about creating a haven of comfort and care for your beloved furry companions. With a team of experienced groomers and trainers and a commitment to excellence, we strive to provide the highest level of grooming services tailored to the unique needs of each dog.


When you bring your dog to DogHaven, we understand your dog is unique and therefore we take the time to listen about your dogs requirements to ensure they receive the treatment and care they deserve. We use a wide variety of premium quality grooming products that are gentle for the skin and coat, ensuring a pleasant and soothing experience. Our dog trainers provide exceptional expertise in a caring and attentive approach, ensuring and tracking the progress of each individual dog.


Services

Dog Grooming

Our professional grooming service ensures that your dog looks and feels their best, with a range of services including bathing, brushing, and nail trimming.

Dog Training

Our dog training programs help your dog learn good manners, basic commands, and address any behavioral issues in a positive and effective way.

Socialization

Daycare provides a chance for dogs to socialize and develop positive behaviors and manners with other dogs and humans.

Beyond our exceptional services, we strive to foster a warm and welcoming atmosphere for both dogs and their owners. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients based on trust, transparency, and open communication. Our friendly staff is always ready to answer your questions, address your concerns, and provide guidance on anything that is on your mind.


Whether you are constantly at work, going on holidays or just need a break, discover the joy of a happy dog at DogHaven. Schedule an appointment today and let us create a grooming experience that will leave your dog looking and feeling their best. Your dog's happiness is our utmost priority, and we are honored to be a part of their care and well-being.

Activities

Access to Modern Facilities

Indoor, Outdoor, Rest and Relaxation areas filled with the latest equipment tailored for all your dogs possible requirements.

Daily Reports & Updates

We want to ensure that you, as the owner, is kept up-to date with whatever services you require so you can go about your day without having to worry.

Food & Grooming Products

As we strive to ensure we use the best products on the market, it is only fair that we offer to sell these products within our facilities to those customers whose dogs reacted well to it.
