Privacy Policy


At DogHaven, we prioritize the privacy and protection of your personal information. Our commitment to maintaining the confidentiality of your data is of utmost important to us. We collect and utilize personal details such as your name, contact information, and specific dog care instructions solely for the purpose of delivering exceptional dog sitting services and ensuring the safety and well-being of your pet. Rest assured that we do not disclose or sell any information to third parties, except where required by law.


Your data is safeguarded through industry-standard security measures, and we retain it only as long as necessary. You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information in our records. Should you have any concerns or questions regarding our privacy practices, please contact us.
